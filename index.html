Our Services

Book Surrender Program

Citizens may voluntarily surrender any printed materials to their local fire station for secure disposal. No identification required.

This service is completely confidential and helps maintain community standards.

Residential Inspection

Certified fire safety inspectors conduct thorough examinations of residential properties to ensure compliance with content regulations.

Inspections are scheduled at resident convenience and conducted with minimal disruption.

Confidential Reporting

Report individuals in possession of unauthorized printed materials through our secure channels.

All reports are handled with discretion to protect community relationships while ensuring regulatory compliance.

Our Mission

The Fahrenheit 451 Fire Department operates under the fundamental principle that societal harmony requires the elimination of divisive and confusing content. Printed materials create inequality, foster dissent, and undermine the collective wellbeing of our society.

"We must all be alike. Not everyone born free and equal, as the Constitution says, but everyone made equal. Each man the image of every other; then all are happy, for there are no mountains to make them cower, to judge themselves against."
- Captain Beatty, Fire Department Chief

By regulating access to printed materials, we ensure that all citizens have equal access to approved information channels, preventing the intellectual elitism and social stratification that historically plagued our society.

Our work protects citizens from the confusion, dissatisfaction, and unhappiness that inevitably results from exposure to contradictory ideas and complex philosophical concepts.
